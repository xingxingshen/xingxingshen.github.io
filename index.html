Hi, I'm Xingxing Shen

I am a postdoc fellow in Rokas lab at Vanderbilt University. I am broadly interested in evolutionary biology, phylogenetics, comparative genomics, and bioinformatics. My curent researches mainly focus on: 1) identifying the factors that lead to phylogenetic incongruence; 2) investigating phylogenetic relationships in animals and fungi using genome-scale data; 3) developing universal nuclear markers for phylogenomics.
